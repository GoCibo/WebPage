Chefs

Our chefs are 'common people' like you and me, like your mom or wife. 'Common people' and not 'professionals' because homemade taste and homemade love can only be found at home. However, we surely have culinary trained professionals to perform a thorough quality check and certify the chefs before getting them on board. Our home chefs cook every bit of that bite you order, completely at home and prepare it with utmost simplicity, quality and homely feel. That's what makes us what we are!
